

# 🌾 FarmerDirect Awareness Website

## 📌 Overview

This is a modern **awareness and research insight website** built around the *FarmerDirect* agricultural platform.
It is designed to promote digital transformation in Sri Lanka’s agriculture sector and present research findings related to farmer profitability, market inefficiencies, and digital adoption.

The website also includes interactive features to engage users and collect research-based insights on digital readiness in farming communities.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Project

* Create awareness about agricultural price gaps in Sri Lanka
* Highlight challenges in traditional farming supply chains
* Promote digital agriculture and direct selling concepts
* Present research-based insights in an interactive way
* Collect data through a digital readiness quiz for analysis
* Showcase how technology can improve farmer income and transparency

---

## 📊 Key Research Focus Areas

* Farmgate vs consumer price differences
* Role of middlemen in agricultural supply chains
* Farmer digital literacy and technology adoption
* Income improvement through digital platforms
* Market access and transparency in agriculture
* Awareness of digital selling and marketing tools
